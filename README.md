---

## 📌 Overview

This project involved designing and implementing a complete digital solution for a tour services company that was operating entirely through manual processes with no centralized systems.

The goal was to modernize business operations by introducing scalable IT solutions that automate customer interaction, streamline data management, and enable online booking and payments.

The project focused on integrating multiple systems into a single workflow to improve efficiency, reduce manual effort, and enhance customer experience.

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

The company faced several operational challenges:

- No online presence for customer engagement  
- Manual booking and payment processes  
- No centralized system to store or manage customer data  
- Limited ability to track leads or customer interactions  

---

## 🚀 Solution Implemented

To address these challenges, three core IT systems were designed and integrated:

---

### 🤖 1. AI-Powered Virtual Assistant (Dialogflow)

- Built and configured a conversational AI assistant using Dialogflow  
- Designed custom intents and responses based on company services and customer queries  
- Created a structured knowledge base covering travel packages, pricing, and FAQs  
- Enabled automated handling of customer inquiries, reducing dependency on manual responses  

---

### 📊 2. Customer Relationship Management (HubSpot CRM)

- Implemented HubSpot CRM to centralize all customer data and interactions  
- Configured contact management, lead tracking, and pipeline visibility  
- Integrated web forms to automatically capture and store customer inquiries  
- Improved visibility into customer activity and sales workflow  

---

### 🌐 3. Website & Booking System with Payment Integration

- Developed a responsive website to provide an online presence  
- Integrated booking workflows to allow users to select travel packages  
- Connected Stripe API to enable secure online payments  
- Embedded forms to automatically send customer data into HubSpot CRM  

---

## 🔗 System Integration

The key focus of this project was connecting all systems into a unified workflow:

- Website → captures user inquiries and bookings  
- Form submissions → automatically stored in HubSpot CRM  
- AI Assistant → handles customer questions and guides users  
- Stripe → processes payments securely  
- CRM → tracks all customer interactions and transactions  

This created an **end-to-end automated system** from customer interaction to payment and data management.
